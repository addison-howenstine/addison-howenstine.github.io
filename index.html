About Me

Hey I'm Addison. I'm a curious guy from New Orleans who loves food, climbing, exploring, the outdoors, space, and a bunch of other stuff.

I study Electrical & Computer Engineering and Computer Science at Duke University where I also enjoy being a member of the Outing Club, rock climbing team, and AERO/AIAA club. You can probably find me studying in 3rd floor CIEMAS, playing bumper pool in my RA room, teaching 3D printing at the Innoation Co-Lab, climbing at the rock wall, or sleeping in a tent in K-ville leading up to the UNC vs Duke basketball game.
