3D Modeled & 3D Printed Cookie Cutters

Recently it struck me that there aren't nearly enough fun cookie cutters in the world. I thought to myself, why has no one made a Harambe cookie cutter yet? So, I made one myself.

I use Adobe Illustrator to draw my design for each cutter and save three separate .svg files to extrude in Fusion 360: a cutting edge, a design press, and a base. I import these in Fusion 360 to extrude the three pieces to different heights. I print the cookie cutters on our Ultimaker 2+ printers at the Innovation Co-Lab, where I work teaching 3D printing.

I should probably start selling these things. Email me if you want to buy one I guess?
